In the recent past a shift to big data emerged. A lot of new non-relational databases raised to handle the new tasks. IBM discovered that every day $2.5$ quintillion bytes of new data are created and $90\;\%$ of the worlds data has been created in the past two years \cite[???]. With more and more applications being deployed to the cloud new technologies are being utilized. A shift from relational (SQL) databases to non-relational (NoSQL) databases is observed.
